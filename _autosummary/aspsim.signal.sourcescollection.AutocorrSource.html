class aspsim.signal.sourcescollection.AutocorrSource(num_channels, autocorr, rng=None)

autocorr is array of shape (num_channels, corr_len)

with autocorr[:,0] being lag 0.

All channels are all independent. Could be extended in the future to accept autocorr of shape

(num_channels, num_channels, corr_len) which could include cross-correlations
